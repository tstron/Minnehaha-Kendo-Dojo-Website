Kendo equipment

Bogu, uniforms, and where to order

Ordering as a beginner

You can order a shinai bag, hakama, and keiko gi as soon as you would like—there is no need to wait. Ask the instructors if you want advice on sizing or style before you buy.

Full bogu (armor) is usually purchased after you have trained for several months and are ready to join bogu practice; we can help you choose when the time comes.

Kendo gear

Brief guide to names and pieces. Men is the helmet, kote the gloves, dō the chest protector, and tare the waist and thigh protection.
